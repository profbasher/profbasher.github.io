What does Proofreading mean?

Proofreading means the process of reviewing the final draft of a piece of writing to ensure consistency and accuracy in spelling, grammar and formatting. Proofreading is the fact that the word is used differently in different places.

It is important to proofread and revise a document before printing and publishing. A proofreader's foremost duty is to check the formatting, page numbers, headers, etc. and ensure no errors are found.

A proofreader will check a document for any kind of grammatical, typographical, or formatting errors.
